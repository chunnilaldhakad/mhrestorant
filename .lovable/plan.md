
# Shree Govindam Masala House - Premium Restaurant Website

## Design Vision
A premium, food-focused website inspired by 28thali.com's elegant split-layout design, featuring warm Indian colors (maroon, saffron, cream, gold accents), smooth animations, and a conversion-driven experience.

---

## Color Palette & Typography
- **Primary Colors**: Maroon (#8B2635), Saffron/Gold (#D4A853), Cream (#FDF6E3)
- **Accent**: Deep Gold (#B8860B), Warm Brown (#6B4423)
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body text
- **Design System**: Rounded cards, soft shadows, warm gradients, smooth scroll animations

---
